Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

This page explains how Syntra Digital handles information related to this site and any contact you initiate.

What we collect

  • Contact details: If you submit the contact form or email directly, we collect the information you choose to share (name, email, and message contents).
  • Basic analytics: Plausible Analytics provides privacy-friendly insights (page views, referrers, device types). It does not use cookies or track individual visitors across sites.

How we use this information

  • To reply to your inquiries and coordinate potential work.
  • To understand how the site is being used so we can improve content and experience.
  • To keep the site functioning securely and reliably.

Sharing and retention

Syntra Digital does not sell or rent your information. Contact details are kept only as long as needed to respond or maintain an ongoing project relationship. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymous.

Your choices

You can request:

  • Access to the information you’ve shared.
  • Corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Deletion of contact details that are no longer needed.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email hello@syntrasolutions.digital.
