Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Syntra Studio (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Form Details: Name, email address, and any information you include in your message.
  • Analytics Data: Non-identifying usage information collected through Plausible Analytics to understand site performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
  • Improve website performance, content, and user experience.
  • Maintain security and prevent spam or abuse.

Data Sharing & Retention

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your contact details are stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of your inquiry. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify individual visitors.

Cookies & Tracking

This site uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly solution that does not rely on cookies or collect personal data.
